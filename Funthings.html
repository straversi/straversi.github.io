Pinophyta Poster

I'm a fan of information, especially when presented on a poster. One may imagine my dismay when I couldn't find a single poster of a taxonomic branch of any part of 'life'. I made it my goal to create one. I found that the Division Pinophyta in Plantae contained a reasonable (about 630) amount of plant species. And so my poster was born. Please enjoy. If you'd like a print, please contact me via email and we could set something up.

Minecraft Adding Machine

If you like logic gates and arithmetic, then this is the fun thing for you. A 5-bit adding machine in Minecraft allows you to add one number to another by flipping levers in two areas. The result is displayed as a binary number represented by lit (or not) lamps. This result is acheived by 5 chained "adders", which consist of AND, OR, and XOR gates. World download is available here: Link

Morse Code Practicer

One thing you may not have known about me is that I am fluent in international morse code. Whether that will ever be applicable is both yours and my best guess. It is fun to tap out secret messages to other people who know the code though, even if the number of people in that category is very small. After finishing learning, I threw together a javascript morse code site, in which YOU TOO can teach yourself morse code, or just practice. Enjoy.
